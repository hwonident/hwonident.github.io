The Walt Disney World

The Walt Disney World is an entertainment complex in Florida, near Orlando.
I have spent a full day in Disney's Animal Kingdom.
Disney's Animal Kingdom is a zoological theme park.
Avatar Flight of Passage is the most popular Animal Kingdom ride for its realistic trip through Pandora.
I really recommend going there and making good memories despite an expensive ticket lol

Universal Studio

The park encourages its guests to "ride the movies" and features numerous attractions and live shows.
I boarded the Hogwarts Express ride in individual cabin compartments just like Harry rode in the movies.
My best ride is Hollywood Rip Ride Rockit.
You can pick the song that you’ll listen to on the ride.
You can have an amazing panoramic view of the park and plenty of twists and turns until you are back at the station.

Hollywood, LA

It was a long slog to the top of the hill.
I took a taxi for a long time and walk over arduous paths.
I walked so much that I was really exhausted before seeing the Hollywood Sign.
But a view of the hill was stunning and I felt invigorated.
It is a little dangerous to take a photo on the cliff,
but you should do.
I'm sure you can get the best photo near the cliff of the hill.
